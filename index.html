Zero-shot Any-to-Any 3D Style Transfer with Gaussian Splatting

Shan Tang, Wenhua Qian*, Huaguang Li Peng Liu Jinde Cao
Yunnan University
*Southeast University
Paper Supplementary Code arXiv

A2AGStylizer is a zero-shot method that enables real-time any-to-any stylization without the need for training.

Abstract

Recent developments in radiance fields have opened exciting opportunities for producing high-quality 3D assets and scenes. The incorporation of style transfer can further promote these 3D creations by infusing them with a variety of artistic styles, thereby enriching creative expression. However, existing 3D style transfer methods often suffer from artifacts, color bias, and lack the flexibility to localize style transfer to specific objects. These limitations hinder their usage in diverse scenarios, such as VR/AR. To address these challenges, we propose A2AGStylizer. This interactive 3D style transfer technique allows users to apply different artistic styles to specific regions of a 3D scene at a rate of 10 frames per second (fps). The method builds on a feedforward 3D style transfer network, incorporating Adaptive Feature Transformation and 3D Color Matching, effectively addressing style inconsistencies and distortions. Additionally, we integrate a 3D segmentation framework into the Gaussian rendering process for precise, localized style transfer. We demonstrate its effectiveness in a range of 3D scenes and styles, showcasing enriched control and customization of 3D creation. Our project and code will be available after acceptance.
